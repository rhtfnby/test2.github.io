SOLUTION APROACH

A simple registration on WSCOIN allows investors easy access to experienced traders
worldwide. Unlike other solutions on the market, WSCOIN only publicizes strategies,
which were actually implemented by the respective trader on their own portfolio. The
platform captures and processes all trades in real-time. These features ensure that
WSCOIN offers followers maximum transparency, considering past trading results. The
result is a bulletproof basis for successful cryptocurrency trading.

WS PLATFORM

To emphasize the most significant features of the platform, this chapter will illustrate the
advantages offered to each user.
4.1 User
There are several user types on the platform:
The Follower, the Trader and the Demo User. Each of the different user types is briefly
defined below.
4.1.1 Follower
Followers are investors who either lack the time to engage in complex market studies
or who are new to trading. The goal of the followers is to invest in cryptocurrencies
as successfully as possible with little effort. Followers log in to the platform, in order
to find suitable traders based on their preferences and strategies and copy them.
Principally, the follower tracks a trader for one week.
4.1.2 Trader
Traders are defined as experienced investors and traders in financial markets. Their
objective on WSCOIN is to maximize their profits, build a large group of followers
and thus maximizing the profits of the whole community. These objectives are
achieved through successful trades and informative feeds.
4.1.3 Demo user
Demo users are essential followers, except with no real capital. They act like
followers but have no influence on the balance sheet of the traders they follow. This
free demo mode is intended to help understanding the platform’s structure and
operating principle, and is a setting that can be freely activated or deactivated at any
time

Trader benefits

The trader’s profile consists of two parts. All relevant data will be gathered in
real-time from the trader and visualized for potential followers automatically in form
of charts, numbers and averages in different time periods. In addition, the feeds,
which are either public or only visible to followers, can be rated by means of likes.
The trader receives detailed statistics on his trades and feeds. Some of these
statistics are public and can be viewed by followers and shared on social media
platforms
